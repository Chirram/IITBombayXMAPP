Welcome to IITBombayX-MAPP(Monitoring Application)

ADMIN

  • MAPP reports will help Administrator monitor the health of IITBombayX.
  • The user can monitor course-wise disk usage, view the registrations for different courses per day, view the enrollments of students under numerous courses and monitor the email server.

Program Manager

  • The program manager can check IITBombayX metrics at the partner institute level.

FACULTY

  • They may take necessary action to improve the results by providing some extra study material based upon individual performances considering grades and attendance of their students.



STUDENT

  • In MAPP, a student is provided with a snapshot of his attendance and grading performance
  • He may evaluate his own grades in different quizzes as well as compare his performance against the average performance.

GENERAL INFORMATION

  • General information reports are open to all individuals who are interested in researching the usage of MOOCs.
  • The reports available are divided into different categories like those based on gender, level of education, age and geographical background.

IITBombayX Monitoring Application (MAPP) is an online application for tracking the IITBombayX deployment and provide quick snapshots of data which various users would be interested in monitoring. It aims to provide feedback to users and allow them to perform action based on the same.
